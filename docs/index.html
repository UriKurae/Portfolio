About Me

My name is Oriol Bernal, a developer that loves developing and learn new techniques to someday create a company that can make unique and fun games.
My goal in the industry is to learn as much as I can so I can develop games that people can enjoy and share good times with their friends.
Right now I am studying at the university, doing a bachelor's degree in videogame design and development and while I am doing that I enjoy doing research by myself at some other subjects that could help me become a better teammate, such as 3D or game engines like Unity.
I also have studied 3D with Maya in a 2 year course and learned Zbrush by myself so I can be helpful in some other areas besides programming, I love 3D and programming as it can be seen in this little description.
The goal of creating this portfolio is so people can see some of the projects I work and enjoy making, and I will keep this updated to see my progress over the years.
